HOW TO GET STARTED?

BEFORE YOU BEGIN, HERE ARE SOME TIPS TO GET YOU STARTED

Create Your Instagram Account

This is one of the most important parts of getting started and staying motivated! By joining the community of like minded Stronger girls, you can stay on track and connect with others on the app! The success rate of girls creating an Instagram account to track their progress increases dramatically. Your username can be anything you want or you can have it pertain to the app!

For example:

@Sia_Stronger, @Stronger_Kaley, @sbg_Heather, @strongbodyLayla, etc. Be sure to use the following hashtag with every post such as #strongerapp so we can easily find yourposts!

Take Your Before Photos

This is a super hard step, but is vital in order to track your progress and your success on the app! These photos will allow you to see your small changes over time so you can notice all of your hard work. We encourage you to take your initial progress photos before you begin the app and then every 4 weeks afterwards. There is a collage feature within the app that is super handy!

To take your progress photos:

  1. Find good lighting natural or in front of a window is always best.
  2. Pose the exact same way and snap a photo of your front, side, and back.
  3. Wear the exact same outfit for every photo: shorts, sports bras, or swimsuit.
  4. Share your progress online!

Join the Community

Within the app, there is a forum where you can chat with other women, ask questions, and post your progress. You can also find your tribe on Instagram by using hashtags #strongerapp, #siacooper, #sbggirls, and #diaryofafitmommy. When you find another girl on the app, follow them and say hello!

Don't forget:

To follow us on @diaryofafitmommyofficial and the Stronger app page as well.

Start Sweating

Now that you have completed the first three steps, it is finally time to crush your first workout in theapp! Select your program and begin with week 1. Be sure to post in the apps message board forum andlet us know how you did and how we can help you stay accountable.

That is it!

Create your account, join the community, snap your progress photos, and start your very first workout. Dont forget to share your progress along the way! You never know who youll inspire.
